About YouTube Slide Extractor

This web application helps you extract slides from educational YouTube videos. It works by analyzing video frames at regular intervals and identifying unique slides based on image comparison and text recognition.

Features:

  • Extract slides from any YouTube educational video
  • Adjust sensitivity parameters for better results
  • Download all extracted slides as a PDF
  • No data is permanently stored on our servers
